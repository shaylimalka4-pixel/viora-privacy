Viora Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Viora is an AI-powered fashion discovery platform designed to help users discover outfit inspiration, explore fashion trends, find similar products, and receive personalized style recommendations.

Protecting user privacy and maintaining transparency regarding how information is collected and used are important priorities for Viora.

Information We Collect

Depending on how users interact with the platform, Viora may collect:

  • Name and profile information
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Style preferences and interests
  • Saved outfits and favorites
  • Images voluntarily uploaded by users
  • Device and usage analytics
  • Interactions with content and recommendations

AI Features and Uploaded Images

Viora uses artificial intelligence technologies to improve the user experience. When users upload images, those images may be analyzed by AI systems to:

  • Identify clothing items and fashion elements
  • Generate outfit recommendations
  • Suggest similar products
  • Improve personalization and style matching
  • Provide fashion discovery features

Uploaded images are processed solely for providing requested features and improving user experience.

How We Use Information

  • Provide personalized fashion recommendations
  • Generate AI-powered style suggestions
  • Improve platform functionality and performance
  • Save user preferences and account settings
  • Enhance content relevance and discovery
  • Maintain platform security and reliability

Third-Party Services

Viora may utilize trusted third-party technologies and service providers, including artificial intelligence services, analytics platforms, cloud infrastructure providers, and content discovery services.

These providers may process limited information necessary to deliver platform functionality.

Data Security

Viora takes reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to help protect user information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Data Sharing

Viora does not sell personal information to third parties. Information may only be shared when required to provide platform functionality, comply with legal obligations, or protect the security and integrity of the service.

User Rights

Users may request access, correction, or deletion of their personal information where applicable under relevant laws and regulations.

Children's Privacy

Viora is not intended for children under the age required by applicable laws. The platform does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect improvements, new features, regulatory requirements, or operational changes.
